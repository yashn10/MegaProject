Why Donate Blood?

We have provided effective, inspirational and motivational guidance on Blood Donation. Blood Donation is a gesture of compassion towards the sufferer or the diseased. Your one donation can save up to three lives.

Who Can Donate?

Almost any healthy individual can donate blood as long as they are eligible and pass all criteria. However, people under age 18 and over 60 should abstain from blood donation. Approach nearby camps or donation centres to get started.
