Our Vision

Blood Buddies is a public donation center with blood donation members in the changing health care system. We provide specialist blood donors and clinical supervision, increasing communication with our members, and high quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment. The extra care of a multi-disciplinary team.

User Testimonials

Since the institution of blood banking, safety for both donors and blood recipients has been continually improved. Because a safe, reliable source of blood is critical to providing effective blood products, blood banks are dependent on the altruistic voluntary donations of citizens. Blood banks place great emphasis on making the donation process pleasant, convenient, and as safe as possible for donors.
